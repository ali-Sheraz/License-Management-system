Artificial Intelligence

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. It has become an essential part of the technology industry.

Mission

The mission of AI research is to advance technology to create machines that can learn, adapt, and potentially improve the quality of human life. AI aims to replicate human decision-making capabilities in an efficient and scalable manner.
